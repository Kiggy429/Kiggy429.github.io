Year is 2123.
Highly advanced technology, androids, etc..
The world while highly advanced has no knowledge of anything beyond humans or the cyber upgraded.
The ones who do know about the supernatural of the world when being a broad term or racist call them The Abnormals. (This spans every race. Though hated by them.)
Due to the nature of how many "Abnormals" exist there is no end to what all exists in the world. (Vampires, Fairies, Shapeshifters, Demons, Angels, Goblins, Elves, Superhuman, and many more noted over the years.)



HERD INFO
H.E.R.D., known as Heroes Emergency Rescue Department, is a top secret government funded organization made up of the "Abnormals."
The organization is a very small group of "Abnormals" formed in three teams.
Murder of Crow: The special recon and stealth squad.
Lion's Pride: ????
The Wolf Pack: ????
They are funded to stop any threat of the "Abnormals" being leaked to the human race and make sure no one abuses their powers.
While minor dabbling is ignored, power imbalance or pure abuse is not. Whatever the higher ups see fit to call upon them for.
Otherwise H.E.R.D. just watches for new budding "Abnormals" so they can guide them a bit if need be or cover up the powers being seen.



Prologue Details
A female android is being assaulted in the back of MC's bar, when he goes to take out the trash.
She reminds him of his sister who died in a car crash so he protects her.
Only to find out she is a newly activated model that is being sought out by a Mafia-type group.
She holds secrets to said group without really knowing it and was owned by the leader, who ends up being an "Abnormal" as well.
