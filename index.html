My Schedule
Sunday: Record/Edit Day
I have a movie night with a friend and otherwise I will likely start using Sunday to record for YouTube, edit any videos for TikTok or otherwise. Ideally I start getting stuff to post later on days I'm not live or doing things.

Monday: Off/Moderator On Twitch/Publishing Day
I mod for people on Monday, you won't find me on Twitch or otherwise but I may start making this a day that I publish stuff on YouTube.

Tuesday: Twitch Streaming
I go live on Twitch, Tuesday's nights (Central Time, USA), usually closer to 11pm or Midnight. (Midnight would officially be Wednesday.) I generally aim to go at least 6 hours, but end time is never really decided. I sometimes end early if I got plans the next day or if I finish a game at like 4-5 hours. I won't start another mid-stream.

Wednesday: Off/Moderator On Twitch/Publishing Day
I rest usually from stream and mod for others on Twitch. As an introvert streaming does take a lot more energy out of me than most probably understand. Though I love it. Another good candidate for upload days.

Thursday: Twitch Streaming
Just like Tuesday, I'm likely to go live at Twitch around 11pm-midnight (Central Time, USA). So sometimes I don't go live until officially Friday in my time zone. Just like Tuesday as well, I aim to go 6 hours minimum though we have gone as long as 10+ hours on a casual stream day.

Friday: Off/Moderator On Twitch/Publishing Day
Recovering from Thursday, and likely modding around Twitch or otherwise. Also another good candidate for uploads to YouTube or otherwise.

Saturday: Off/Moderator On Twitch
Nothing. I plan to take Saturday as my own day. I will still be around to mod on Twitch or such but this day will not focus on anything but me time.

Exceptions:
I have insomnia and depression. I can't always control sleep or drops in moods. I'm not gonna make myself sick to be live or so on.
A new game release, I may move a stream day to another day if I want to play the game on the actual launch. Like when Resident Evil 4 Remake released I played it back to back streams until I beat it.
I don't want to burn out either and I think sometimes I do too much, too fast or put it in my head that I must do this or that. I hate how spotty I have been lately with streams. Though I'm learning my mental/physical health IS important.
